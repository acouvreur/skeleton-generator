Skeleton Generator Project

Features

Setup your project in a minute

Configuring your project can be a pain, especially for complex architectures. Skeleton Generator enable you to setup and configure a modular project. The result is a ready to use web application.

Generate a flexible skeleton code

From a XML representation of your model, Skeleton Generator will generate robust, flexible and scalable code. Best practices are deeply studied to bring you the best from the most popular java frameworks.

Populate your database easily

Skeleton Generator can help you creating a project from scratch or migrating an existing project. With its population plan feature, you can feed your database with data from various datasources. You can also mount an "in memory database" for unit/integration tests.

Why use Skeleton Generator

Save time

With Skeleton Generator, you will save much time while configuring many files such as Maven pom files, Spring and Hibernate configuration files, Loggers, Transactions, Aspects handling, Internationalization, ...
You will also get rid of very repetitive tasks such as writing your SQL definitions, your model with Hibernate mappings, your DAO, Services, MVC for CRUD operations, ...

Improve your application quality and maintainability

With a Model Driven Development approach, Skeleton Generator produces robust design patterns that enables you to make your model change many times without breaking what you have written by yourself. During the whole life cycle of your application, you will keep a very high level of quality by avoiding frequent errors due to manual changes.

Control your data

With its population feature, you use your own services to populate your database. This will ensure you that business rules are observed. It also encourages Test Driven Development by providing simple tools to setup a testing environment with an "in memory database" and control the datas you inject in.
